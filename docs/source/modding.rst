Modding
====================

Mods serve as the basis for content CollabXR.
Each mod is an `Asset Bundle`_ which contains one or many `Prefabs`_ that are annotated for CollabXR.

You can use the :doc:`CollabXR mod packager<modpackager>` to upload your mod.
You can also :ref:`load a repository<loading-your-repository>` (whether yours or someone else's) to give you access to those mods in the spawn list.

.. _Asset Bundle: https://docs.unity3d.com/6000.0/Documentation/Manual/AssetBundlesIntro.html
.. _Prefabs: https://docs.unity3d.com/6000.0/Documentation/Manual/CreatingPrefabs.html
